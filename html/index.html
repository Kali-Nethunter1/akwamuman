Akwamumam Senior High School

Akwamumam Senior High School is a premier educational institution located in Akwamu in the Eastern Region, Ghana. It is one of the oldest and most respected schools in the country, having been established in 1955. The school has a reputation for excellence, rigorous curriculum that focuses on academic achievement and personal development. The school boasts a diverse student population, with students from all over Ghana and abroad. The school's mission is to empower students to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens by providing them with a quality education. The school provides a a supportive environment that promotes learning, respect, and collaboration. The school offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including music, sports, and clubs, which foster creativity, teamwork, and leadership. Akwamuman Senior High School is committed to equipping its students with the knowledge and skills to succeed in their future endeavors. In short, Akwamuman Senior High School is an institution where students can learn and grow in a safe and nurturing environment. The school is predominantly a boarding school having facilities for both boys and girls. The facilities available are not adequate and over utilized. And also provides day facilities for those in the vicinity who would want to be day students. The vision statement of Akwamuman Senior High School, Akosombo, is “To build a reputable school that will produce students of high caliber who will be useful to themselves, their families and the nation’’.

Mission statement is to:

  • Train students to acquire academic excellence in their various programmes, principally with the aim of entering tertiary institutions;
  • Produce disciplined students who shall be imbued with a high sense of responsibility;
  • Uphold the school’s motto “Discipline and Knowledge’’ in their quest to face the realities of life, and
  • Work and serve the nation as best as they can.


History

The school was named Akwamumam by the chief of Akwamu Traditional area, late Odeneho Kwafo Akoto II in 1969 with the help of other traditional leaders. In January, 1972, it was absorbed into the public education system. Located at New Powm, about 1km from Atimpoku.

SCHOOL CREST AND MOTTO

The School Crest had been thoughtfully carved out to depict a picturesque from the ideas of the school. The Adomi Bridge and the Volta River beneath the bridge depict the locale of the school, which can be found about six hundred (600) meters from the Adomi Bridge, which is the heart of the Akwamu State. The opened book with lighted torch depicts knowledge that students are to acquire through assiduously applying themselves to their books. The two lions holding aloft the lighted torch depicts an aspects of Akwamu History, that is, ‘bravery’ which had made the school to earn the accolade “GREAT AKWAMUSEC’’ SCHOOL MOTTO The school’s motto is DISCIPLINE AND KNOWLEDGE.


Facilties

The school has many facilities thay help the students to learn. It has about fifty(50) Classroom, one Library, one Science Laboratory, two Computer Laboratory, two Farmland, one Medical Care centre (called the 'sickbase'), a Telephone Facility for both MTN and Vodafone, its also have a Dining Hall and a Centeen for both broading and day students. The school has a football field, a volley ball pitch, basket ball pitch and parks with garden- chairs for relaxation.


Courses

Akwamumam Senior High School provides courses which helps students major in their field.

They provide courses like:

  • General Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Elective Mathematics, Elective ICT
  • Home Economics: General Knowledge in Art, Management in Living, Foods and Nutrition, Biology
  • Business (Accounting Option): Business Management, Business Mathematics & Principles of Costing, Principles of Accounting, Elective Mathematics, Economics
  • General Arts: Literature in English, Economics, History, Government, Elective ICT, Elective Mathematics, French, Christian Religious Studies, Geography
  • Visual Arts: General Knowledge in Art, Graphic Design, Ceramics, Elective Mathematics, Economics
